Bio

Dana Bassett is a writer, editor, producer, problem solver, logistical coordinator, fundraiser, shapeshifter, talker, listener and a sensitive, yet demanding, soul. Born in Miami Florida under the Leo moon, Dana currently in the Pilsen neighborhood of Chicago with her husband, sister, their fish and her sister’s cat, Norbu. She has directed organizations such as The Artists’ Cooperative Residency and Exhibitions Project (ACRE), Borscht Film Festival, and is the current Executive Producer of Bad at Sports, the longest running arts podcast (in the world?). Her writing has been published in outlets such as The Smudge, Hyperallergic, The Miami Rail and The Seen.
